# Jobseeker's Allowance — Ireland

A means-tested weekly payment for people who are unemployed or working reduced hours.

## Eligibility

- Must be aged 18 or over and under 66.
- Must be fully unemployed, or working 3 days a week or less.
- Must be available for full-time work and genuinely seeking work.
- Must be "habitually resident" in Ireland (living in Ireland with close, ongoing links to the country).
- Means-tested: income must be below a set threshold, which varies by household circumstances.
- Identity must be verified to SAFE Level 2 before the claim can be paid.

## How to apply

Register for SAFE Level 2 verification and apply at a local Intreo Centre or Social Welfare Branch Office, or online via MyWelfare.ie.
