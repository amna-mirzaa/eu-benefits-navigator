# Ingreso Mínimo Vital (Minimum Vital Income) — Spain

A guaranteed minimum income benefit for individuals and households in economic vulnerability.

## Eligibility

- Applicant (or the household unit) must have legal and effective, continuous residence in Spain for at least one year before applying — this one-year requirement is waived for minors added via birth, adoption, family reunification, foster care; victims of human trafficking/sexual exploitation; and women who are victims of gender-based violence.
- Benefit holder must be at least 23 years old (some exceptions for minors with dependent children or care-leavers).
- Income and assets must be below set thresholds — "economic vulnerability" is defined as average monthly income (over the prior year) being at least €10 below the guaranteed IMV amount for the household's size/type.
- The household unit ("unidad de convivencia") must have existed for at least 6 months.
- Applicant must not be an administrator of a commercial company.

## How to apply

Use the official simulator/application at imv.seg-social.es, or apply through Social Security (Seguridad Social) offices.
