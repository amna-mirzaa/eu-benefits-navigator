# Fuel Allowance — Ireland

A payment to help with home heating costs during the winter fuel season (roughly late September to April), paid to one person per household.

## Eligibility

- Must live in Ireland; cannot claim if heating costs are already covered in full as part of accommodation.
- **Age 66+**: qualifies without necessarily receiving another qualifying social welfare payment. Means limit: single person up to €524/week, couple up to €1,048/week.
- **Under 66**: must be receiving a qualifying social welfare payment (from Ireland, the EU, or another qualifying country) and pass a means test. Average gross weekly income limit: up to €534/week single, €1,068/week for a couple.

## How to apply

Apply online via MyWelfare.ie before the start of the Fuel Allowance season (late September).
