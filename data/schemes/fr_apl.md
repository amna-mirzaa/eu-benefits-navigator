# APL (Personalized Housing Assistance) — France

APL reduces the amount of rent or mortgage payment owed by low-income tenants and buyers.

## Eligibility

- Available to tenants, co-tenants, declared sub-tenants of a "conventioned" (rent-agreement) dwelling, or property purchasers who used a conventional loan (PC) or social housing loan (PAS).
- The housing must meet decency standards and minimum-occupation conditions.
- Means-tested: eligibility ceilings vary by household composition and location (Paris/large cities vs. rural areas have different thresholds). All household members' resources over the last 12 months are taken into account.
- Both French and foreign residents (with valid residence status) can apply, including non-EU residents with a valid residence permit.

## How to apply

Apply exclusively online via the Caisse d'Allocations Familiales (CAF) website (caf.fr), or via the Mutualité Sociale Agricole (MSA) for those under the agricultural regime.
