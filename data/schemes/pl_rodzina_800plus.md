# Rodzina 800+ (Family 800+) — Poland

A universal child-raising benefit paid per child up to age 18, regardless of family income (successor to the "Family 500+" programme; the per-child amount rose to PLN 800/month from 1 January 2024).

## Eligibility

- Must live in Poland.
- For Polish citizens: all children under 18 are entitled, regardless of parental income (universal, not means-tested), since 1 July 2019 (as 500+, now 800+).
- For foreign residents with children in Poland: eligible if they hold a residence permit for highly qualified employment, have access to the Polish labour market (with some exceptions for short-term work permits, student residence permits, or visa-based work), or hold a temporary residence permit for intra-corporate transfer purposes.
- Citizens of countries with bilateral social-security agreements with Poland (including Canada, the US, Australia, Moldova, South Korea, and Ukraine) are also eligible.

## Amount

PLN 800 per month per child, no income test.

## How to apply

Apply online via the ZUS (Social Insurance Institution) Platforma Usług Elektronicznych (PUE ZUS), or in person.
