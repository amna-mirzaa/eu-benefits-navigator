# Assegno di Inclusione (Inclusion Allowance) — Italy

Italy's minimum-income support scheme, targeted at households containing at least one "fragile" member.

## Eligibility

- The household must include at least one vulnerable member: a minor, a person with disability, a person aged 60+, or someone in a certified disadvantaged condition enrolled in territorial socio-health assistance programmes.
- Citizenship/residency: applicant must hold Italian citizenship, EU citizenship with residence rights, a long-term EU residence permit (for third-country nationals), or refugee/international-protection/stateless status.
- Applicant and all benefiting household members must have resided in Italy for at least 5 years total, with the last 2 years continuous.
- Means test: household ISEE (equivalent economic situation indicator) must not exceed €10,140/year (2025 threshold).
- Conditional on participation in a personalised social and employment support/inclusion programme.

## How to apply

Apply via INPS online channels (inps.it) after obtaining/confirming an ISEE certificate.
