# Rendimento Social de Inserção (Social Insertion Income) — Portugal

Support for individuals and families in extreme poverty, combining a monthly cash benefit with social/professional integration guidance.

## Eligibility

- Income test: if living alone, monthly income must be below €247.56 (2026); for families, the threshold scales with household composition up to the maximum RSI value for that household size.
- Asset test: movable assets (bank deposits, stocks, bonds, savings certificates, other financial assets) must not exceed €32,227.80.
- Residency: EU/EEA citizens or citizens of countries with free-movement agreements need legal residence in Portugal; citizens of other countries need at least one year of legal residence; refugees need legal residence status.
- If unemployed and able to work, applicants must register at the local job centre.
- Applicants must sign a formal "insertion contract" committing to work availability, training, or another appropriate integration activity.

## How to apply

Apply through Segurança Social (seg-social.pt) or a local social security office.
