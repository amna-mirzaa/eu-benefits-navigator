# Zorgtoeslag (Healthcare Allowance) — Netherlands

A contribution toward the mandatory Dutch health insurance premium and compulsory excess, for people on lower incomes.

## Eligibility

- Must be at least 18 years old and hold mandatory Dutch healthcare insurance.
- Income test (2026): annual income must not exceed €40,857 if single, or €51,142 combined if you have a benefit partner.
- Asset test (2026): asset limit is €146,011 for individuals, €184,633 combined for partners.

## Amount

Up to €129/month for single-income households, up to €246/month for households with a fiscal partner (2026), scaled by income.

## How to apply

Apply online via Mijn Toeslagen on the Belastingdienst website using DigiD.
