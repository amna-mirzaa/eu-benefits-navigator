# Huurtoeslag (Rent Benefit) — Netherlands

A monthly contribution toward rent for low-income tenants.

## Eligibility

- Must be 21 years or older (age limit lowered from 23 to 21 as of 2026), rent an independent living space, and have a rental agreement.
- The applicant, any benefit partner, and co-residents aged 18+ must hold Dutch nationality or have legal residence status in the Netherlands.
- All residents must be registered at the address with the municipality (BRP registration).
- Rent must actually be paid (provable via bank statements).
- Income test: income of the applicant, partner, and co-residents must not be too high (income ceilings updated annually).
- Asset test (2026): roughly €38,479 for a single person, €76,958 for a household with a partner.
- As of 2026 the maximum-rent ceiling for eligibility has been abolished.

## How to apply

Apply online via Mijn Toeslagen on the Belastingdienst/Dienst Toeslagen website using DigiD.
